Who we are

CCS Readymix is one of the largest ready-mix concrete production and supply company in Bangalore, Karnataka. We are a subsidiary of the ever growing CCS is a trusted brand by experts in construction industry since 2011. We serve a comprehensive blend of private and public sector establishments. We produce high-quality and high-performance concrete for projects in all vertical, including petro-chemical, infrastructure, hospitality, real state, energy, pipeline and many others. The vast customer base we have worked with over the years is a testimony of our spirit and hardwork. In order to meet the ever-growing demand in Bangalore, we expanded our reach by launching facilities in Bangalore in the year 2011. The quality of our products and services entails our capability and dedication to serve our customers. We invest great resources in setting the most advanced technologies and expertise, in order to live up to the high standards of CCS that embodies strength, reliability and innovation.
